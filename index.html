About

I'm Yumi, CEO and Co-Founder of LEAD.app, where we build knowledge-sharing infrastructure for enterprises. Before LEAD, I was the founding Country Manager for Meitu.

My current research at Columbia University's Information and Knowledge Strategy program focuses on the Organizational Intelligence Loop (OIL), a framework for making enterprise AI systems that actually understand how organizations work.

I write about the intersection of knowledge management, people analytics, and AI adoption. Most of what I publish here starts as a question I can't find a good answer to.

Based in San Francisco. Previously in Tokyo and Shanghai.
